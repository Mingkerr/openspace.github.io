Who we are:

Open Space is a property company, launched in 2015

What we do:

Open Space provides a home for students and growing companies in London

How we do it:

Open Space understands the changing needs of the different students that need a place to work, as well as the companies. We actively adapt and manage buildings to create an environment for maximum efficiency and output

Follow the 3 easy steps:

Find a space: Discover a unique space to work and study. Simply search by location and filter based on availability and amenities

Book and confirm: Book your space, make a payment securely on OpenSpace's trusted platform and we'll email you your receipt and booking confirmation

Check in and work: Your host will be expecting you on the day of check-in. Show your booking confirmation and enjoy your experience
